'Enehana - Computer Science and Coding - Club

'Enehana - Computer Science and Coding - Club (or 'Enehana Club for short) is Punahou School's computer science and coding club, where everyone is welcome to join, regardless of their techspertise. I am one of the co-founders along with Josh Lee ‘22, Liam Hutchison ‘23, and Esther Chan ‘24. 'Enehana means technology in Hawaiian, and we thought that this would be a fitting name for our club so we are able to connect with our home. Our goal is to create a safe and creative space for those who are interested in all things Computer Science to explore and discover and develop various areas of interest.

Leading this club has become a part of me and something that I love. I have been able to develop my programming skills, as well as a way to give back to my community, meet new friends, and teach others. It has developed my passion to help others learn different topics in computer science.
