Intro to Artificial Intelligence

Intro to Artificial Intelligence was an exploratory course post-COVID 2020. During this course, we worked with the company AI Club to learn different methods of artificial intelligence and its ethicality, as well as how they connect to us in ways we might not expect. AI Club's goal is to teach and empower children to learn AI and realize their passions through creative applications of their learning. In addition, they plan to help the next generations of kids learn about AI and imagine the future with new technology.

Throughout this course, we learned how to create different AIs and use the language python to enhance them. This course was a six-week long, and at the end, we had to do a culminating project of all of our learnings.


Final Project:

Name: Age Group Prediction

AI to guess if you are a “baby,” “child,” “teen,” “adult,” or “elderly.”

My culminating final project was an AI that would be able to determine your age group by using an image, whether you are a baby, child, teen, adult, or elderly. Using a database of images, I used Python and the AI methods we learned to create the AI.
