Building a PC

I had the opprotunity to build my first computer when I was 11 years old. My dad thought that it would be a good idea to learn how to build a computer, rather than buying a new one. I enjoyed having the opprotunity to spend time with my dad, while also developing a love for the way that computer parts work harmoniously together.

Throughout the last couple years, with finnancial reason, I have rebuilt and added new parts to my computer. Although not necessary, I enjoyed being able to connect with those who also were interested in computers and learn from them as well.
