About Me!

My name is Lindsay Minami. My greatest joys in high school so far have been being able to build my skills in coding and computer science, as well as helping my community with my new skills. Some examples include starting our school's Computer Science and Coding Club, being a part of Girls Who Code sisterhood, and helping my community be safe on the internet. I want to continue to use my technical skills to help others and create environments that are safe and inclusive. This portfolio, which I have coded as a website, showcases the work that I have done throughout high school but more importantly, it illustrates how I have used these skills to work with and help others. Right now, I am searching for a place where I can continue to help others, as well as challenge myself to learn more about technology. I hope you will take the time to look through my portoflio and discover how I can be a strong contributor to your community.

Service, Clubs, and Extracurriculars

'Enehana - Coding and Computer Science - Club

‘Enehana - Computer Science and Coding - Club (‘Enehana for short), is a place for those with interests in all things computer science to come together and collaborate on projects. No experience is required to join and everyone is welcome. As one of the co-founders, our goal is to provide opportunities for everyone interested in CS.

Further Information Here!

Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code (GWC) is a non-profit organization that promotes inclusivity for women in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math). As the founder of the chapter at Punahou, I want to promote a safe place and environment for everyone to have equal opportunities to learn coding outside of the classroom.

Further Information Here!

Cyber Safe Seniors

Cyber Safe Seniors is a nonprofit organization started by my friend Esther Chan ‘24. As a member of her board (Secretary), our mission is to mitigate the negative effects of cybercrime against our kūpuna (elders) by educating them about safe cybersecurity practices through our comprehensive, easily accessible, and completely free online content.
