About Me!

Hi! My name is Lindsay Minami and my dream is to one day become a software developer. I’ve loved programming and working with computers from a young age. I hope that you can learn a little about me, how I’ve grown, and the work that I’ve done throughout high school. I’m grateful for all the friends, mentors, and teachers that I’ve had throughout the many years; they have pushed me to learn more and create better projects. Below, you can find some of the coursework and projects that I have done (including creating this website).

    Quick Facts:
  • Co-founder and leader of the Punahou ‘Enehana - CS and Coding - Club
  • Founder of the Girls Who Code (GWC) chapter at Punahou
  • Completed 6+ semesters of CS work
    My Aspirations:
  • Explore and Learn
  • Make New Connections and Friends
  • Learn Leadership
  • Give Back
  • Teach Others
